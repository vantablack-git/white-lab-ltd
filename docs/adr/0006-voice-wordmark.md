# ADR 0006: Voice and Wordmark

Status: Accepted

## Context

White Lab must avoid promotional crypto hype, fake decentralization claims, and misleading veToken language.

## Decision

**Wordmark:** `White Lab` — thin, slightly italic Inter. No standalone logo icon in canonical surfaces.

**Voice rules:**

- State status plainly: testnet candidate, audit pending, mainnet blocked
- Describe governance lock vault as weighted and non-decaying, never as veCRV vote escrow
- Prefer "control plane", "manifest", "handover", "obligation", "timelock" over hype terms
- Do not claim audited, production-ready, or mainnet-ready until gates close
- Separate marketing narrative from operational truth in docs and UI ribbons

## Consequences

- Website and console use `.wordmark` styling
- Status ribbons and footers repeat calibrated claims
- Turkish pages are translations of canonical English claims, not alternate promises
